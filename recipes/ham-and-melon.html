Prosciutto e Melone

An easy and refreshing starter featuring cantaloupe and cured ham.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cantaloupe
  • cured ham slices

Steps:

  1. Slice the cantaloupe into even wedges, removing the skin and seeds.
  2. Wrap each wedge of cantaloupe with one slice of cured ham.
  3. Serve cold.
